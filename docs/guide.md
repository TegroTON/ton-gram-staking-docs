# Guide: stake · unstake · claim

Everything happens at **https://tegro.finance/staking** with any TON wallet (Tonkeeper, Wallet in
Telegram, MyTonWallet, etc.).

## 1. Stake

1. Open https://tegro.finance/staking and connect your wallet.
2. On the **Stake** tab, enter an amount of **TON** (≥ the pool minimum).
3. Confirm — you'll see the estimated **stgTON** you receive at the current rate.
4. Sign the transaction. stgTON appears in your wallet and starts earning immediately.

## 2. Unstake

1. Switch to the **Unstake** tab.
2. Enter the amount of **stgTON** to redeem.
3. Sign the transaction. Your stgTON is burned and the contract issues you a **withdrawal voucher**
   for the TON value (minus the 0.5% redeem fee).
4. The voucher appears under **My withdrawals** with a **72-hour** countdown.

## 3. Claim

1. After the 72-hour timer ends, the **Claim** button activates next to your withdrawal.
2. Click **Claim** and sign — your TON is sent back to your wallet at the locked-in amount.

That's the full cycle: **stake → earn → unstake → (72h) → claim**.

## Tips

- Your **stgTON balance stays constant** while its value in TON grows — that's how the yield is paid.
- The withdrawal amount is **fixed at unstake time**; the 72-hour wait doesn't change it.
- Prefer instant liquidity? You can trade stgTON on the market instead of unstaking, subject to
  available liquidity.
- Always verify you're on **tegro.finance** and that the contract matches
  [contracts.md](contracts.md).
