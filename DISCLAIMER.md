# Disclaimer & risks

This documentation is provided for **informational purposes only**. It is **not** financial,
investment, legal or tax advice, and nothing here is an offer or solicitation.

Staking and DeFi carry risk. Before using Tegro staking, understand at least the following:

- **Variable yield.** The target APY (up to ~12%) is **not guaranteed** and can change with network
  and market conditions.
- **Smart-contract risk.** Despite independent review and testing, no smart contract is ever
  risk-free.
- **Market risk.** The value of TON and stgTON can fluctuate.
- **Withdrawal delay.** Exiting via unstake takes a **72-hour** unbonding period before you can claim.
- **Liquidity risk.** Trading stgTON on the open market is subject to available market liquidity.

**Do your own research (DYOR).** Only stake what you can afford to risk, and always verify contract
addresses against [docs/contracts.md](docs/contracts.md) on a TON explorer before interacting.

or reliance on this documentation.
